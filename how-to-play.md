# How to Play

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Open BNB City and connect a BSC-compatible wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Rabby, etc.). Make sure you have BNB for gas fees and deposits.

## Step 2: Buy Land

Before building, you need to own a land plot. The city mayor sells plots as the city grows outward from the center.

**Land types:**

| Plot | Size | Price | Available Tiers |
|------|------|-------|-----------------|
| Mini | 1×1 | 0.001 BNB | T0, T1, T2 |
| Standard | 1×1 | 0.005 BNB | T3, T4 |
| Large | 2×2 | 0.025 BNB | T5, T6 |
| Premium | 3×3 | 0.125 BNB | T7 |

Plots can only be purchased next to roads. Early players get central plots — the most prestigious locations.

## Step 3: Build a Business

Choose a building tier and category (Residential, Commercial, or Industrial). Pay the deposit — your building starts generating yield immediately.

**Example:** You build a T3 business for 0.01 BNB on your standard 1×1 plot. Total entry cost: 0.015 BNB (0.01 deposit + 0.005 land).

## Step 4: Compound or Claim

Every 12 hours, your building accumulates earnings. You have two options:

### Compound (Recommended)
Reinvest earnings back into your building. This:
- Increases your principal (bigger daily income)
- Boosts your activity meter by +15%
- Helps you reach your cap faster
- Gives your referrer a 10% bonus

### Claim
Withdraw earnings to your wallet. This:
- Sends BNB to your wallet (minus claim tax)
- Drains your activity meter by -15%
- Resets the 12h cycle

**Optimal strategy:** Compound for 7-10 days, then claim. Repeat.

## Step 5: Complete the Cycle

Each building has a maximum payout cap. Once reached, the building completes its cycle. You can then demolish it and build a new one on the same plot, or sell the plot to another player.

## Activity Meter (Battery)

Your building's activity level affects its earning rate:
- **100% = maximum earnings**
- **50% = half earnings**
- **0% = no earnings**

Activity recovers passively at +10% per day. Compounding boosts it by +15%. Claiming drains it by -15%.

**Tip:** Frequent claims without compounding will gradually lower your activity. Keep compounding to maintain peak performance.

## 12-Hour Cycle

- Earnings accumulate for exactly 12 hours, then stop
- You must compound or claim to start a new 12h cycle
- If you don't log in for 3 days, you only earn for the first 12 hours — the rest is idle time
- This encourages regular interaction, but there's no penalty for waiting
