Think of building tiers like investment tiers at a brokerage. Each one offers a different risk-reward profile, and the right choice depends on your budget, goals, and how actively you want to play.

## Which tier is right for you?

### Starter tiers (T0--T2): The high-ROI play

These are the "scrappy startup" tiers. You put in very little, but the percentage returns are the highest in the game.

- **Rate modifier: x1.10 to x1.20** -- your buildings earn 10--20% faster than the baseline
- **Cap modifier: x1.18 to x1.25** -- your payout cap is 18--25% higher than baseline
- **Best for:** Testing the waters, learning the mechanics, building on a budget

**Example -- T0 building in Epoch 0:**
- You deposit 0.001 BNB ($0.60). After 10% fee, your working capital is 0.0009 BNB.
- Daily earnings at 6% rate: ~0.000054 BNB/day ($0.032/day)
- Gross cap: 0.001 x 0.9 x 3.0 x 1.25 = 0.003375 BNB ($2.03)
- With 50 compounds (+50% cap bonus): 0.005063 BNB ($3.04)
- That's a **5x gross return** on your deposit with active play

The percentage returns are incredible. The absolute numbers are small -- but that's the point. You can learn the entire system for pocket change.

### Mid-range tiers (T3--T4): The balanced approach

This is where most experienced players operate. Meaningful absolute returns with still-strong percentage performance.

- **Rate modifier: x1.00 to x1.05** -- close to the base rate
- **Cap modifier: x1.05 to x1.15** -- solid cap extension
- **Best for:** Players who understand the system and want real income

**Example -- T4 building in Epoch 0:**
- You deposit 0.05 BNB ($30). Working capital: 0.045 BNB.
- Daily earnings at 5% rate: 0.00225 BNB/day ($1.35/day)
- Gross cap: 0.05 x 0.9 x 3.0 x 1.05 = 0.14175 BNB ($85)
- With +50% cap bonus from compounding: 0.2126 BNB ($127.58)
- **Net after claim tax (~13%): ~$111** on a $35 total investment

That's roughly a 3x net return with active play, on a $35 investment.

### Premium tiers (T5--T7): The whale play

Big deposits, big absolute profits, lower percentage returns. These tiers generate serious daily income in dollar terms.

- **Rate modifier: x0.55 to x0.85** -- earning rate is lower, by design
- **Cap modifier: x0.80 to x1.00** -- tighter caps protect the pool
- **Best for:** Larger investors who want meaningful daily cash flow

**Example -- T6 building in Epoch 0:**
- You deposit 1 BNB ($600). Working capital: 0.9 BNB.
- Daily earnings at 3.5% rate: 0.0315 BNB/day ($18.90/day)
- Gross cap: 1 x 0.9 x 3.0 x 0.80 = 2.16 BNB ($1,296)
- With +50% cap bonus: 3.24 BNB ($1,944)
- **Net after claim tax: ~$1,691** on a ~$627 total investment

Nearly $19 a day in passive income from a single building.

## Why the rate differs by tier

This is a deliberate design choice that keeps the game fair for everyone:

- **Small players** get higher percentage returns to compensate for their smaller capital. A T0 player earning 6%/day on $0.60 gets proportionally more than a T7 player earning 2.75% on $3,000.
- **Large players** get lower rates but massive absolute profits. Their buildings also have tighter caps, which means they extract less from the pool relative to their deposit -- keeping the ecosystem healthy.

This is how BNB City avoids the "rich get richer" problem that kills most DeFi games. Everyone has a viable path to returns.

## The cap bonus: rewarding active players

Every time you compound, your building's payout cap grows by +1%, up to a maximum of +50%. This is huge:

- A T4 building with base cap of $85 becomes a $127 building after 50 compounds
- That's 50% more profit just for logging in daily

The cap bonus is the game's way of saying: "We reward players who show up." Passive investors get the base return. Active compounders get significantly more.

## Building visuals

When you build, you pick a visual category -- Residential (houses, apartments), Commercial (shops, offices), or Industrial (factories). This is purely cosmetic and doesn't affect earnings, but it makes the city look alive.

Your building evolves visually as you compound:
- **Fresh build** -- construction animation
- **Active** -- lights on, windows glowing based on productivity
- **Developed** (3+ compounds) -- enhanced visuals
- **Maximum** (7+ compounds) -- premium look
- **Completed** -- distinctive style when the cap is reached

## The full building lifecycle

```
Buy land -> Build (deposit) -> Compound daily -> Claim profits ->
-> Cap reached -> Demolish (free) -> Build again or sell the plot
```

Each cycle is an opportunity to reinvest at a higher tier, diversify across multiple plots, or take profits off the table. You're in control of your strategy at every step.
