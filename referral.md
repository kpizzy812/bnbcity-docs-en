# Referral System

## How It Works

Invite friends to BNB City and earn a percentage of their deposits and compounds. Referral rewards are automatic and on-chain.

## 3-Level Referral Tree

When your referral builds a new business, you earn:

| Level | Reward | Example (0.01 BNB deposit) |
|-------|--------|---------------------------|
| Level 1 (direct invite) | 2% | 0.0002 BNB |
| Level 2 (invite of invite) | 1% | 0.0001 BNB |
| Level 3 (3rd degree) | 0.5% | 0.00005 BNB |

Rewards are calculated from the **full deposit amount** (before the 10% dev fee). Your referral's building cap is not affected -- referral payouts come from the pool.

## Compound Bonus

When your Level 1 referral compounds (reinvests), you earn **10% of the compound amount** in your referral balance.

This is why the incentives are perfectly aligned:
- **The system** wants compounds (less outflow)
- **The player** wants compounds (better returns)
- **The referrer** wants compounds (10% bonus)
- Everyone benefits from the same action

## Sink Referral Bonus

When your Level 1 referral purchases any boost or sink, you earn **50% of the sink payment**. This applies to all sinks: Auto-Action, Productivity Recharge, Compound Boost, Claim Shield, Epoch Lock, Landmark, and Batch Compound.

**Example:** Your referral buys a Compound Boost for a T4 building (cost: 20% of 0.05 BNB = 0.01 BNB). You receive 0.005 BNB in your referral balance.

This makes active referrals who use boosts very valuable.

## Referral Balance

All referral earnings go to an **internal balance** inside the contract, not directly to your wallet.

**You can use it in three ways:**

### 1. Build a New Business (Best Option)
- 0% deposit fee (money is already in the contract)
- 100% becomes real deposit (vs 90% with fresh BNB)
- ~11% higher cap than regular building
- Money stays in the contract

### 2. Compound Into Existing Building
- 0% fee
- Adds to your building's principal
- Does NOT increase cap (but cap bonus from compound count still applies)
- Money stays in the contract

### 3. Withdraw to Wallet
- **20% tax** (stays in the pool)
- Only option that removes BNB from the contract
- Least efficient use of referral balance

## Referral Registration

- Your referrer is set permanently on your first deposit
- Cannot be changed later
- Cannot refer yourself
- No circular referrals (A->B->A)

## Getting Your Referral Link

Your unique referral link is available in the Referrals section of the game UI. Share it with friends -- when they connect and build their first business through your link, they're permanently linked to you.

## Summary of Referral Income Sources

| Source | Amount | Levels |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Deposit | 2% / 1% / 0.5% | L1, L2, L3 |
| Compound | 10% of compound | L1 only |
| Sink purchase | 50% of payment | L1 only |
