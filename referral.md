# Referral System

The referral system in BNB City isn't an afterthought -- it's a serious income stream. Invite players, and you earn a percentage of everything they do: deposits, compounds, and boost purchases. The incentives are perfectly aligned: the more successful your referrals are, the more you earn.

## What you earn from referrals

### On deposits (3 levels deep)

When anyone in your referral network builds a new business, you earn a cut:

| Level | Your cut | Example (someone deposits 0.05 BNB / $30) |
|-------|----------|-------------------------------------------|
| Level 1 (you invited them directly) | 2% | 0.001 BNB ($0.60) |
| Level 2 (they invited someone) | 1% | 0.0005 BNB ($0.30) |
| Level 3 (third degree) | 0.5% | 0.00025 BNB ($0.15) |

These rewards come from the pool -- they don't reduce your referral's earnings or cap in any way.

### On compounds (Level 1 only)

Every time your direct referral compounds, you earn **10% of the compound amount**. This is where the magic happens:

- Active players compound daily
- Each compound grows their principal (meaning future compounds are bigger)
- Your 10% cut grows along with their success

**Example:** Your referral has a T4 building earning ~0.00225 BNB/day. They compound daily. You earn ~0.000225 BNB/day ($0.135/day) from this one player, and it increases as their principal grows.

### On boost purchases (Level 1 only)

When your direct referral buys any boost, you earn **50% of the purchase price**. This applies to every boost in the game: Auto-Earn, Compound Boost, Claim Shield, Epoch Lock, Productivity Recharge, Landmark, and Batch Compound.

**Example:** Your referral buys a Compound Boost for their T4 building (0.01 BNB / $6). You instantly receive 0.005 BNB ($3) in your referral balance. That's a 50% cut -- from a single purchase.

If your referral is an active player who buys boosts regularly, this becomes a substantial income stream on its own.

## Why the incentives work

Think about what the referral system rewards:

- **You want your referrals to deposit** -- you earn 2% on every deposit
- **You want them to compound** -- you earn 10% of every compound
- **You want them to buy boosts** -- you earn 50% of every purchase
- **The game wants the same things** -- deposits fund the pool, compounds keep money in the system, boosts generate revenue

Everyone wins when players are active and engaged. There's no tension between your interests and the system's health. This is by design.

## Your referral balance

All referral earnings go to an **internal balance** inside the smart contract. You have three ways to use it:

### Option 1: Build a new business (best ROI)

- **0% deposit fee** (normally 10%) -- because the money is already in the contract
- Your entire referral balance becomes real deposit (100% vs. 90% from fresh BNB)
- This means roughly **11% higher cap** compared to building with external BNB
- The money stays in the contract, keeping the pool healthy

This is the optimal play. You're essentially getting a building with a higher cap for the same cost.

### Option 2: Compound into an existing building

- **0% fee**
- Adds to your building's principal (bigger daily earnings)
- Does NOT increase the base cap (but cap bonus from compound count still applies)
- Another great option if you already have a building you want to grow

### Option 3: Withdraw to your wallet

- **20% tax** (stays in the pool)
- The only option that actually removes BNB from the contract
- Least efficient -- you lose 20% vs. reinvesting at 0% fee

**The math is clear:** Reinvesting gives you 100% of the value plus a cap bonus. Withdrawing gives you 80%. Most experienced players reinvest.

## Earning potential scenarios

**Casual referrer (5 active L1 referrals, each with one T4 building):**
- Deposit bonuses: 5 x 0.001 BNB = 0.005 BNB ($3) one-time
- Daily compound bonuses: 5 x ~0.000225 BNB = ~0.001125 BNB/day ($0.68/day)
- Monthly compound income: ~$20/month
- Plus whatever they spend on boosts (50% cut each time)

**Active referrer (20 L1 referrals, mix of tiers, plus L2 and L3 network):**
- Deposit bonuses across all levels can be substantial
- Daily compound bonuses from 20 active players: $2--5/day
- Boost purchases: sporadic but significant (a single T6 Compound Boost purchase nets you $60)
- Monthly income: $100--200+ depending on network activity

**Power referrer (large network with some whale players):**
- A single T7 player depositing 5 BNB earns you 0.1 BNB ($60) on the deposit alone
- Their daily compounds could generate $1--2/day for you -- from one person
- Scale this to multiple active T6/T7 players and the numbers get serious

## How to get started

1. Open the **Referrals** section in the game UI
2. Copy your unique referral link
3. Share it -- when someone connects through your link and makes their first deposit, they're permanently linked to you
4. Referral relationships are set on first deposit and can never be changed

## Key details

- You cannot refer yourself
- No circular referrals (A refers B refers A)
- Referral bonds are permanent and on-chain
- Earnings are credited instantly when the action occurs
- There's no limit on how many people you can refer
