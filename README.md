# BNB City

**Build a business -- take x3.**

Imagine a shared pixel city on the blockchain where every building is a real income-generating asset. You pick a plot, build a business, and watch it earn BNB every single day. No middlemen, no waiting for approvals, no "trust us" -- just a smart contract doing math on BNB Smart Chain.

## Why players are choosing BNB City

**The math is in your favor.** A Tier 0 building in Epoch 0 has a gross payout cap of 3.75x your deposit. After all fees, that's roughly **2.9x net return** on your money. With active compounding, the cap bonus pushes it even higher -- up to **+50% on top**.

**You can start with less than $2.** The cheapest entry (T0 + Mini land) costs just 0.003 BNB -- about $1.80. No minimum investment barrier, no gatekeeping.

**Early players win big.** BNB City uses a deflationary epoch system (think Bitcoin halving). Right now, in the earliest epochs, the base daily rate is 5% and the payout cap is 3.0x. Both shrink as the city grows. The earlier you build, the better your deal -- permanently.

**Everything is on-chain and unchangeable.** One smart contract, no admin keys, no proxy upgrades, no "we updated the terms." The rules are set in code and nobody -- not even the team -- can change them after deployment.

**It's a real community.** Everyone builds in the same city. You can see every player's buildings on the shared isometric map. Your business has a physical location, neighbors, and a reputation.

## Multiple ways to earn

- **Building yield** -- your buildings generate daily income automatically
- **Compounding** -- reinvest earnings to grow your principal and boost your cap
- **Referrals** -- earn 2% / 1% / 0.5% on your network's deposits, plus 10% on their compounds and 50% on their boost purchases
- **Land speculation** -- buy plots early at prime locations, sell later on the P2P marketplace
- **Marketplace trading** -- buy and sell buildings with active income streams

## Quick start (5 minutes)

1. Get a wallet ([Rabby](https://rabby.io), [MetaMask](https://metamask.io), or [Trust Wallet](https://trustwallet.com))
2. Load some BNB (even 0.01 BNB is enough to get started)
3. Connect to BNB City and grab a land plot
4. Build your first business
5. Come back every 12 hours to compound -- and watch your income grow
