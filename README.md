# BNB City

**Build a business -- take x3**

BNB City is a trustless on-chain isometric city-builder game on BNB Smart Chain (BSC). All players build in one shared pixel city. Each building generates daily yield through deflationary redistribution mechanics with an adaptive rate and epoch-based halving.

## Why BNB City?

- **Shared world** -- everyone builds in one city, visible to all
- **Self-regulating economy** -- adaptive rate adjusts yield based on pool health
- **Tier differentiation** -- 8 building tiers with unique rate and cap modifiers
- **Deflationary model** -- 7 epochs reduce rewards over time (like Bitcoin halving)
- **Boosts and sinks** -- optional upgrades to enhance your buildings
- **Fully on-chain** -- single immutable smart contract, no proxies, no admin keys
- **Transparent fees** -- all fees are visible and verifiable on-chain

## Quick Start

1. Connect your wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Rabby)
2. Buy a land plot from the city mayor
3. Build a business on your plot
4. Compound daily to grow your income
5. Claim your earnings when ready
6. Activate boosts to maximize efficiency
